We can help fixing these if you choose to delegate this service

  • Simplified, focused navigation: cut it from 12+ items to 5-6 clear top-level sections. Remove the 11-item Solutions sub-menu and replace with a simple drop-down or clean mega-nav.

  • Strong, focused hero section: replace the generic stock photo with a specific ROI claim ("Cut your waste costs by 25%"), one main CTA ("Book Free Audit"), and the Enterprise Ireland badge placed above the fold.

  • Consistent brand identity: define a clear colour palette, typography (one sans-serif for body, one accent for callouts), and tone of voice. Apply it consistently across all pages.

  • Professional header: replace personal mobile numbers with a central company number and a clean "Contact" CTA. Add the company logo to the header. Fix the broken social media icons with inline SVGs.

  • Copy corrections and rewrite: fix all known typos ("Technoogoy", "Governence", "anditment"). Rewrite jargon-heavy sections with specific outcomes and real numbers. Move the best claims to hero position.

  • SEO title tags and meta: rewrite the homepage title to target "Waste Management Ireland" and "CSRD Compliance Ireland". Set an og:image across all key pages so social shares show a proper preview.

  • Lightweight hero (no Slider Revolution): replace the Slider Revolution WordPress plugin with a plain HTML/CSS hero block to dramatically improve page load speed and Core Web Vitals scores.

  • Case study showcase: put Abcon, Gilead, Borrisoleigh Bottling, and Maryborough House Hotel front and centre on the homepage as a trust-building section near the top.

  • WCAG AA accessibility: fix colour contrast issues across the site. Make sure keyboard navigation, skip links, semantic HTML, and ARIA labels work throughout.
